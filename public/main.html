Fuel Economy Tips

💨

Maintain Proper Tire Pressure

Under-inflated tires can lower gas mileage by about 0.2% for every 1 PSI drop in pressure.

🛣️

Drive at Moderate Speeds

Fuel economy peaks at around 50-60 mph on most vehicles. Going faster increases drag.

🔄

Regular Maintenance

Regular oil changes and air filter replacements can improve gas mileage by up to 10%.

❄️

Reduce AC Usage

Using the AC can reduce fuel economy by up to 25%. In mild weather, try using the vents.
